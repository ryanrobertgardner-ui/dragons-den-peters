Step 1 of 6
Assign Your Roles

Split the group into two sides. Read each role out loud so everyone knows what they're doing.

2–3 people
The Pitchers
You'll get a random business idea in a moment. In 2 minutes, figure out: What problem does it solve? Who buys it? Why will it make money? Then pitch it in 90 seconds — no script, just talk.
Everyone else
The Dragons
Listen to the pitch, then fire questions. Think like an investor: Is this real? Who's the customer? What could go wrong? At the end, vote: Invest, Maybe, or Pass.

Step 5 of 6
Dragon Q&A

Dragons — about 3 minutes. Fire your toughest questions. Use these to get started:

"What happens if a big company copies your idea tomorrow?"
"How much would you charge — and why that price?"
"Who is your biggest competitor right now?"
"What could go badly wrong in year one?"
"Why would a customer choose you over a free option?"
